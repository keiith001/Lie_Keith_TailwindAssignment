Project Overview

Project Overview

The goal of this project is to make a company profile website for Eternity Futures. It is a new investment and trading company in Indonesia.

As the UI/UX designer I also make the UI guidlines following the brand identity. Making all the website UI for then coded and implemented by the programming team.

Inspiration Phase

Inspiration

In this section I will start with Inspiration phase which will understand the problem, target audience, and design challenge based on data received from client.

Problem Faced

The client is a company called Topgrowth Futures which works on Forex and Commodity trading field. Most of their clients are older people ranged from 30 to 50 years old with a high economic status. However they want to expand their business to catch the interest of younger audiences. Then they create a new brand called Eternity and need a Brand Activation for younger ones to join them.

Client

“We need to activate our brand with visuals and flows that could engage young audiences to be interested in trading without worry”

Me

  • “How might we activate the brand in this digital era?”
  • “We could try several possible solution for the first step! It might need a base for information through digital media, the more it accessible for users the better it is.”
  • “We want to target young generation to be aware of eternity and try to join it”
  • “It has to be eye catching and modern so that young people could be engaged and remember the brand”

Defining Target Audience

  • In this step, I try to define the target audience by interviewing the client
  • Then I also ask opinions from my creative director about the psychological traits the audience have
  • Finally with the base in mind, I try to ask around such as my friends which fits in the category about their preferences and what are their motivation in start trading
